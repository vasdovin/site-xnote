XNote Organiser

Are you working on a project? Planning a vacation? Or storyboarding your writing?

XNote Organize - lists unlimited ideas and tasks, visually, in an easy and fun flowchart!

  • Quickly create notes and create links between each to create a diagram of your idea or project.
  • Visualize the entire project on a single canvas.
  • Group dependent tasks together and easily prioritize each step.

XNote Organizer is the simplest and fastest way to lay out your notes and then gather your great ideas into a coherent plan. You can establish relationships between notes one-to-many or one-to-one. Develop a script, plan, or project in the easiest note planning tool, today!
